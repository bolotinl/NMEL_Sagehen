* more details available in the corresponding Campbell manuals for each sensor

* I thought there were usually only 3 soil moisture sensors: 10, 30, 50 cm, ask Gary about the 4th


VWC_1_Avg: 10 cm soil volumetric water content
EC_1_Avg: 10 cm soil electric conductivity 
ST_1_Avg: 10 cm soil temperature 
P_1_Avg: 10 cm soil permittivity
PA_1_Avg: 10 cm soil period average (raw sensor output)
VR_1_Avg: 10 cm soil voltage ratio
VWC_2_Avg: 30 cm soil volumetric water content
EC_2_Avg: 30 cm soil electric conductivity 
ST_2_Avg: 30 cm soil temperature 
P_2_Avg: 30 cm soil permittivity
PA_2_Avg: 30 cm soil period average (raw sensor output)
VR_2_Avg: 30 cm soil voltage ratio
VWC_3_Avg: 50 cm soil volumetric water content
EC_3_Avg: 50 cm soil electric conductivity 
ST_3_Avg: 50 cm soil temperature 
P_3_Avg: 50 cm soil permittivity
PA_3_Avg: 50 cm soil period average (raw sensor output)
VR_3_Avg: 50 cm soil voltage ratio
TT_C: Surface temperature from IR radiometer (true temperature) 
SBT_C: Sensor body temperature from IR radiometer
FuelM_Avg: Fuel moisture
PA_uS_Avg: Fuel moisture sensor period average (raw sensor output)
